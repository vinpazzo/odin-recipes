KitKat Milkshake Recipe

Description

This is a KitKat Milkshake made from the finest vanilla icecream and KitKats, they go hand in hand to make one of the most beautiful combinations known to man kind, once you try one of these I guarantee you will not be as satisfied with another milkshake for a while.

Ingredients

  • Vanilla icecream
  • Milk
  • KitKat chunky bar

How to make

  1. Grab a glass of your choice
  2. Add a generous amount of vanilla to your glass
  3. Break the KitKat chunky up with hands or smash with object until it easily crumbles
  4. Apply small amount of milk and ster with a FORK (not spoon) until you find the right consistancy you'd like
  5. Apply half of the broken KitKat chunky to the glass
  6. Use fork to stab the chunky pieces down to the bottom
  7. Add second half of the KitKat to the glass and do the same as the step before
  8. Ster until it is all mixed together
  9. Either eat with a spoon or the fork or drink it, either way it doesn't matter
  10. Make more until you're so full you can't walk!
