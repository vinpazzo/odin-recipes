Pitta Pizza Recipe

Description

These pitta pizza look and taste delicious, they are made from all healthy ingredients and taste just as if not better than your normal dominos pizza! They don't make you feel groggy and awful after you have eaten it, and you certainly don't feel that regret afterwards. So with these pitta pizzas you will have the best of both worlds and still be able to enjoy yourself while staying healthy. Below is a list of all the ingredients which are in this delicious dish.

Ingredients

  • Wholemeal pitta bread
  • Tomato puree
  • Cheese
  • Garlic
  • Oregano
  • Chilli

How to make

  1. Preheat oven to 180 degrees
  2. Wrap a tray in tinfoil
  3. Place your pittas on the tray making sure they all fit on properly
  4. Open your tomato puree and spread an even amount on all of the pittas, make sure to cover the pitta in the puree, however don't drown it
  5. Grate your cheese and place an even amount on the pittas, you can have as much or as little as you like
  6. Sprinkle a tiny amount of garlic ontop of the cheese on each pitta
  7. Do the same with the oregano
  8. Do the same with the chilli powder
  9. Put in oven for 15-18 minutes
  10. Take out and enjoy the pitta pizzas!
